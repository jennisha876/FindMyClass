Information

Welcome to "Find My Class," your go-to solution for navigating and optimizing your university experience. This will guide you through the features and functionalities of our web application, to ensure you make the most of your time on campus.


Getting Started

1.1 System Requirements

- Ensure you have a device with a modern web browser.
- The application is optimized for desktop and mobile devices.

1.2 Creating an Account

- Click on the "Sign Up" button to create your user profile.
- Fill in the required information and create a secure password.
- Confirm your email address to activate your account.


Main Features

2.1 Interactive Campus Map

- Explore the campus map to get an overview of your surroundings.
- Use the search function to find specific buildings or locations.

2.2 Service Directory

- Access information on academic resources, dining options, and events.
- Stay updated on library hours, cafeteria hours, and campus activities.
- Explore comprehensive details to enhance your university experience.

2.3 Personalized Experience

- Create and customize your user profile for a personalized journey.
- Receive notifications about campus events and announcements.


Using FindMyClass

3.1 Home Screen

- After logging in, you'll land on the home screen.
- Navigate through the menu to access different features.

3.2 Interactive Campus Map

- Click on the map icon to explore the interactive campus map.
- Use the search bar to find specific buildings or locations.

3.3 Service Directory

- Access the service directory from the menu.
- Explore different categories for comprehensive information.
- Click on specific services for detailed information.

3.4 Personalized Experience

- Access your user profile from the menu.
- Customize your profile settings and preferences.


Troubleshooting and FAQs

4.1 Common Issues

Check your internet connection. Ensure your browser is up to date. Contact support for technical assistance.

4.2 Frequently Asked Questions

Contact support for personalized assistance.



Thank you for choosing "Find My Class." We hope this user manual enhances your experience on campus. If you have any further questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our support team. Happy navigating!
