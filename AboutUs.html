About Us

Welcome to "Find My Class," the cutting-edge solution designed to transform the university experience for students and faculty alike. In the wake of the global pandemic, the challenges faced by university communities have become more pronounced than ever. As we strive to return to "normal life," our web application addresses the unique difficulties encountered by university students, particularly those who are foreign to the campus environment.

Our Mission

At "Find My Class," our mission is to simplify campus life and enhance the university experience for all. We understand the struggles of navigating a large and complex university campus, especially during these challenging times. Our application is a holistic solution aimed at providing seamless navigation and access to essential campus services.

Key Features

Interactive Campus Map:

1. Easily view your campus map.
2. Search for specific buildings or locations.

Service Directory:

1. Access comprehensive information on academic resources.
2. Explore dining options and cafeteria hours.
3. Stay updated on event schedules and campus activities.

Personalized Experience:

1. Create user profiles for a personalized experience.
2. Save favorite locations for quick access.
